SECURITY CLEARANCE

All personnel must maintain appropriate clearance levels. Unauthorized access to restricted areas will result in immediate termination and memory suppression.

CONTAINMENT BREACH

In the event of a containment breach, follow emergency protocols immediately. Evacuate to designated safe zones and await MTF response teams.

03

INFORMATION SECURITY

All information regarding Black Stone operations is classified. Disclosure to unauthorized personnel is strictly prohibited and punishable under Protocol ██.
